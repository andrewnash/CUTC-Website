Founding partner and Creative Director of Dc Design House Inc., an award-winning advertising and design company in St. John’s, NL;

Two-term President of the Business Association of Newfoundland and Labrador; Director with the St. John’s Board of Trade; Co-founder of Mental Health Advocacy group It’s Mental; Board member of the Boys & Girls Clubs St. John’s; Mentor; Marketing partner and host of TEDx St. John’s yearly event;

St. John’s Board of Trade Volunteer of the year; MusicNL Volunteer of the year; Rotary Northwest Aidan Maloney’s Young Professional of the Year (‘15); 21Inc. Top 40 under 40 emerging leaders in Atlantic Canada (‘15); GGCLC Alumni;

Host of Out of the Fog – a local matters weekly show on Rogers and Eastlink networks across NL; Singer/songwriter, musician (709); Event Emcee; Starred in EntrepreneurNL, a six-episode lifestyle-documentary series about life in business and community in St. John’s, Newfoundland and Labrador appearing on NTV, Rogers TV, Eastlink and streaming channels.

Dc is forever looking forward to creating, contributing and communicating ideas and energies that help make our city and province great!
