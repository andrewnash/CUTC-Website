Joe Teo

Co-Founder and CEO HeyOrca

Joe Teo is the Co-Founder and CEO of HeyOrca, a social media planner built for marketing agencies to simplify content planning and client approvals. He was recently featured on Twitter Canada’s list of 20 Canadian tech startup founders that are using the social media tool to its fullest. Joe is inspired by the challenges and satisfaction of helping marketing teams around the world work smarter.

HeyOrca has been named one of the top 20 B2B tech companies in Canada by G2 Crowd. More than 400 agencies use HeyOrca to engage in real-time collaboration and become an indispensable partner for each of their clients.
