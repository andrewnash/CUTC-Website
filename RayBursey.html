Ray Bursey

CEO GHOST TECHNOLOGIES

Ray’s wealth of experience spanning many fields provides his organizations strategic foundation. Successful endeavors in HVAC Automation, Control programming, Security and Telecom sectors add to over a decade in electrical and electronic consulting. Since 2015, Ray has been an active participant within the Blockchain ecosystem providing venture capital to data-center start-ups, overseeing digital asset management while consulting and educating individuals. With a keen eye for sales and business development and extensive experience in technology management, Ray’s ability to connect current events with future trends positions his ventures for repeated success.

Ray has a Master’s in Technology Management and a Post Graduate in Quality Management from Memorial University of Newfoundland, Canada. He also has a diploma in Electrical Engineering Technology. He is the director of Ghost Technologies Inc., a premier Blockchain and Cryptocurrency Consulting organization. He is was also co-founder and CMO of BlockLAB in Labrador and Modular Block Mining in British Columbia. He has given presentations to all levels of government as well as to public and private organizations on all topics in the blockchain and cryptocurrency ecosphere.
