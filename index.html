FAQ

What are the costs of attending?
Tickets for admitted delegates will cost $15 (or $10 each if you apply with a friend!).
Who Can Attend?
High school, undergraduate and graduate students from all across Newfoundland and Labrador can attend.
What’s different at CUTC then say, at a hackathon?
At CUTC, it’s not just about hacking. Sure, you can get your hands dirty and create things; however, you also get to learn from the people in the trenches--entrepreneurs, pioneers in technology and the ones who build and maintain the tech we use everyday.
What am I getting for my money?
We’ve been working hard to cut prices as much as possible to make sure cost doesn’t get in your way. Tickets will only cost you $15. You get access to keynotes, workshops, challenges, as well as mini-events, & a delicious lunch and dinner
